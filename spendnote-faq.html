Getting Started

SpendNote is a simple cash receipt management tool for teams. It helps you track internal cash movements, create printable receipts, and maintain visibility across your team — all without the complexity of accounting software. Perfect for petty cash, employee advances, and team handoffs.
Getting started is easy! Sign up for a free trial (no credit card required), create your first cash register, and you can start creating receipts immediately. The whole process takes less than 2 minutes.
No, SpendNote is not an accounting or tax tool. It's designed specifically for internal cash tracking and creating proof of handoff. The receipts you create are for internal documentation purposes — they're not invoices and shouldn't be used for tax purposes.

Features

Yes! With Starter and Pro plans, you can customize receipt templates including your company logo, custom wording, and even translate to different languages. The only element that can't be changed is the disclaimer that clarifies this is not a tax document.
You can share receipts in multiple ways: email them directly from SpendNote, download as PDF and share, or print them for physical handoff. All team members with access can also view receipts in real-time from the dashboard.
Absolutely! The Free Trial includes 1 register, Starter includes 2 registers, and Pro gives you unlimited registers. This is perfect for tracking cash at different locations, events, or departments.

Billing & Plans

The free trial gives you 14 days or 20 receipts (whichever comes first) to try SpendNote. You get 1 cash register, 1 user, basic receipt templates, and PDF reports. No credit card required to start.
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no questions asked. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. Your data remains accessible for export even after cancellation.
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure payment processor. For annual plans, we can also arrange bank transfers for larger organizations.

Security & Privacy

Yes! All data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest. We host on EU servers, are GDPR-ready, and follow industry best practices for data security. Your financial data is never shared with third parties.
Absolutely! You can export all your receipts and transaction history as CSV files at any time. PDFs of individual receipts can also be downloaded. Your data belongs to you.
Our servers are located in the European Union, ensuring compliance with GDPR and other EU data protection regulations. This provides strong privacy protections for all users.
